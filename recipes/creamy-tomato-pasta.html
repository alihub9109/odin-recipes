Creamy Tomato Pasta

Description

This creamy tomato pasta is the ultimate comfort food—quick, simple, and delicious! It features a rich tomato sauce with garlic, cream, and parmesan.
Perfect for busy weeknights or a cozy weekend dinner.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz pasta of choice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili flakes (optional)
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil, chopped (for garnish)

Directions

  1. Step 1: Boil pasta in salted water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water and drain.
  2. Step 2: Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Step 3: Stir in crushed tomatoes and chili flakes. Simmer for 8-10 minutes.
  4. Step 4: Add heavy cream, Parmesan, and cooked pasta. Toss well, adding pasta water if needed. Garnish with basil and serve.
