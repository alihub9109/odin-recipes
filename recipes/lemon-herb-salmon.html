Lemon Herb Salmon

Description

This lemon herb salmon is fresh, zesty, and full of flavor.
It’s oven-baked and perfect for a healthy dinner that's ready in under 25 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried dill
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon slices and parsley for garnish

Directions

  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Step 2: Place salmon fillets on the baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  3. Step 3: Sprinkle with garlic powder, thyme, dill, salt, and pepper.
  4. Step 4: Bake for 12–15 minutes until salmon flakes easily. Garnish with lemon slices and parsley.
