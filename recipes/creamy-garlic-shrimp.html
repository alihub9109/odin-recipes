Creamy Garlic Shrimp

Description

This creamy garlic shrimp recipe is restaurant-quality but super easy to make at home.
It’s rich, garlicky, and perfect served over rice or pasta.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Directions

  1. Step 1: Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add shrimp and cook until pink, about 2–3 minutes per side. Remove and set aside.
  2. Step 2: In the same pan, add garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Step 3: Stir in cream, broth, and Parmesan. Let simmer until thickened, 3–4 minutes.
  4. Step 4: Return shrimp to the pan and coat with sauce. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with parsley and serve.
